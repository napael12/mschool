Everything your music studio needs

Whether you teach piano in your living room or run a full music academy, keeping track of lessons, assignments, and schedules can get overwhelming fast. MuSchool was built by musicians, for musicians — a clean, no-nonsense tool that keeps you organized so you can focus on what matters: the music.

It's completely free, no subscription required. Just sign up and start teaching.

Lesson Scheduling

A beautiful calendar view keeps all your lessons organized. Book new lessons in seconds by clicking any open slot.

Assignments

Attach practice notes and homework to every lesson so students always know what to work on before the next session.

Resource Library

Build a shared library of sheet music, recordings, and video links. Attach any resource to a lesson with a single click.

Easy Rescheduling

Life happens. Reschedule or cancel lessons effortlessly and automatically notify all participants by email.

Credit Tracking

Set a credit cost per lesson and track balances per student. Apply or refund credits with one click — no spreadsheets needed.

Lesson History & Notes

Every lesson keeps a running comment thread between teacher and student — a permanent record of what was covered.

Built for Teachers

Run your studio your way. Create lessons, assign materials, manage multiple students, and track lesson credits — all from a single dashboard. When you need to cancel or reschedule, MuSchool handles the notifications so you don't have to chase anyone down.

Designed for Students
