Music Lessons,
Simplified.

MuSchool is a free platform built specifically for music teachers and students — giving you everything you need to run your lessons smoothly, all in one place.

  • Open MuSchool →
  • See Features

Lesson Scheduling

A beautiful calendar view keeps all your lessons organized. Book new lessons in seconds by clicking any open slot.

Assignments

Attach practice notes and homework to every lesson so students always know what to work on before the next session.

Resource Library

Build a shared library of sheet music, recordings, and video links. Attach any resource to a lesson with a single click.

Easy Rescheduling

Life happens. Reschedule or cancel lessons effortlessly and automatically notify all participants by email.

Credit Tracking

Pay however works for you — cash, Venmo, whatever you prefer. MuSchool just keeps a simple running tally of lesson credits per student, so everyone stays on the same page without storing any payment details.

Lesson History & Notes

Every lesson keeps a running comment thread between teacher and student — a permanent record of what was covered.

Ready to simplify your music lessons?

Join teachers and students already using MuSchool to stay organized, stay in sync, and spend more time making music. It takes less than a minute to get started — and it's free.
